Brazilian Lemonade

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 4 cups cold water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. In a large pitcher, combine lemon juice and sugar.
  2. Stir until the sugar is completely dissolved.
  3. Add cold water and salt.
  4. Mix well and taste for seasoning.
  5. Fill glasses with ice cubes and pour the lemonade over the ice.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
