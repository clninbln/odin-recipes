Tonno Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Canned tuna
  • Sliced red onions
  • Olives (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Distribute canned tuna and sliced red onions on top of the cheese.
  6. Add olives if desired.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from oven, let cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
