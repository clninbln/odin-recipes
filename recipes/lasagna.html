Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  2. Brown ground beef in a large skillet.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Bake at 375°F for 25 minutes.
